# Haunted Museum
### My First Attempt at a Roblox Game

---

## Background

During this time, a lot of horror games were popping up on Roblox. Games like Piggy, the Mimic, Doors, and just a bunch of hidden horror gems that were never seen. And so I was inspired to try and make my own. I didn't make it too far but I'm proud of what I was able to come up with. It also taught me that game developers are some of the most resilient people out there because somedays you just won't feel like doing anything but they'll come back to continue working.

## Premise

The premise of my horror game would be that the player was stuck inside of a museum at night (kind of similar to Night at the Museum) and the exhibits would come to life. The player would be aided by some of the exhibits and other's would be malicious entities. The player would eventually dig deeper into the museum's past and the origin of everything. It was obviously very ambitious for a first project and so as expected it never took off fully.
