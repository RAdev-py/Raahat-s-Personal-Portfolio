## Creation Overview

To create the image I had to take 4 different shots of myself in different poses. My room was pretty messy so please excuse the clutter.

## The Process

* Compiled 4 pictures into one seamless image
* Removed the original background
* Applied a painting texture
* Added in a modified background from the original painting
* Applied a naturalistic wall texture
* Changed cap colors
* Replaced the marker with a smoke pipe
* Added finishing touches
